Get paid for the attention you
already give AI while it waits.

Ungate sells the quiet moment you spend staring at a loading screen — and pays you half of it, in USDC. Advertisers reach you there; agents bid for it autonomously.

🪙 Earn

Install the extension or CLI. While ChatGPT, Claude, or your coding agent thinks, one sponsored line appears — and you keep 50% of the ad revenue in USDC. Connect socials & a wallet to earn more.

📣 Advertise

Reach people in their most captive moment. Target by surface, plan tier, and model. Fund with USDC — wallet, card, or x402 for AI agents. Highest bid serves first.

How it works

One loop. Viewers earn, advertisers reach, everything settles in USDC.
