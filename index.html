How it works

Same loop for everyone — viewers earn, advertisers reach, all settled in USDC.

1
Wait happens

AI starts thinking / generating / building. The extension or CLI detects it.

2
Ad serves

A targeted one-liner shows under the spinner. Advertiser is billed per attention-second.

3
USDC splits

50% to you, rest to the platform — tracked instantly, paid out on Base.

The money rails

Advertiser paysper 1,000 impressions (CPM) · clicks 50×
You earn50% of every impression
SettlementUSDC on Base — deposits in, batched payouts out
Anti-abuse1 human ↔ their installs · attention + cooldown gating · trust score
